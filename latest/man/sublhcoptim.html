Optimised subset of LHC Sampling Plan

Optimised subset of LHC Sampling Plan

Generate an optimised subset of an existing plan. The optimisation of the subset is based on a genetic algorithm.

Functions

LatinHypercubeSampling.subLHCoptim — Method.
function subLHCoptim(X,n::Int,gens;popsize::Int=100,ntour::Int=2,ptour=0.8)

Produce an optimized Latin Hyper Cube with n sample points from a subset of points in X. Optimization is run for gens generations.
